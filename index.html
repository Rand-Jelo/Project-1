About Me

I’m Rand Jelo, a web developer dedicated to crafting efficient and engaging digital experiences. I focus on building functional, user-friendly websites and enjoy exploring new technologies and techniques to enhance my work. Thanks for stopping by!

Projects

Digital Clock

A clock that shows the current time in hours, minutes, and seconds, updating every second. It works like a live digital clock on the screen, providing the current time in a simple, easy-to-read format.

Interactive Quiz Game

A fun, interactive quiz where users answer multiple-choice questions and get a score based on correct answers. The quiz keeps track of progress and displays a final score at the end, making it engaging for learning or testing knowledge.

Weather Finder

A weather app that lets users enter any city to see the current weather conditions. It shows temperature, humidity, and a brief description of the weather. It’s perfect for anyone wanting quick weather updates without searching through multiple sources.

Recipe Book

A digital recipe book where users can add, view, and organize their favorite recipes. Each recipe includes a title, ingredients list, and cooking instructions, making it easy to keep track of meals. It’s perfect for anyone who wants a simple way to store and access recipes in one place.
